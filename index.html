Forecasting Stock Returns & Volatility

Estimation of an asset valuation model and stock beta, as well as the analysis of return forecasts for three CAC 40 stocks, using time dependence and conditional volatility models with Python. I combined these two approaches by first modeling the series dynamics with an ARIMA, then applying a GARCH on the residuals to capture changing volatility. The objective was to start with simple models to identify the optimal one, incorporating major events such as the COVID-19 crisis. The data used comes from Yahoo Finance.
